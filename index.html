São José do Rio Preto, São Paulo, Brazil

São José do Rio Preto is a city in the northwest of São Paulo, an important regional center for commerce, services, health, and education. Founded in 1852, the local economy was initially based on coffee cultivation, later diversifying into sectors such as agribusiness and livestock. The city is home to renowned universities, such as UNESP, and has a developed urban infrastructure, with highlights including cultural events like Expo Rio Preto.
